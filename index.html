Why Every School & College Needs a Website?

The Digital Age is Here – Don’t Get Left Behind!

In today’s world, having a strong online presence is not a luxury—it’s a necessity! The University Grants Commission (UGC) has made it mandatory for all educational institutions to have a website with at least basic details.

UGC Guidelines: Websites are Mandatory for Institutions

As per the UGC’s new rules, every school, college, and university must have an official website that includes:

✅ Basic institute details (Name, Address, Contact)

✅ Course & Admission details

✅ Faculty information

✅ Accreditation & Affiliation status

✅ Fee Structure & Scholarships

Ignoring this rule may result in lower student enrollment, lack of transparency, and trust issues.
